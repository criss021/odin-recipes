PB&J

Description

This PB&J sandwich is no ordinary sandwich; it's a perfect blend of creamy peanut butter and sweet jelly that will take you back to your childhood with every bite! It's incredibly easy to make, requiring just three simple ingredients, and is a delightful treat any time of the day. Whether you're in need of a quick lunch or a satisfying snack, this classic combination is sure to become your go-to favorite.

Ingredients

  • peanut butter
  • jelly of your liking
  • bread

Steps

  1. Grab 2 slices of bread and put them in the toaster till you get a perfect golden toast.
  2. Grab your jar of peanut butter and spread healthy amount of peanut butter on to one of the bread slices.
  3. Grab your jar of jelly of your choice and spread a light amount of jelly on to the other bread slice.
  4. Grab the peanut butter bread slice and put it on top of the jelly slice and boom you have the perfect pb&j.
